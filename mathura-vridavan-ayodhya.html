Package Overview

Experience the divine charm of Mathura, Vrindavan & Ayodhya with a peaceful, well-planned 3-day spiritual tour, covering the birthplaces and key temples of Lord Krishna and Lord Ram.

The 3 Days Mathura Vrindavan and Ayodhya Tour from Delhi is a spiritually uplifting journey connecting the sacred lands of Lord Krishna and Lord Ram. This beautifully curated itinerary covers the holy temples of Mathura, the vibrant ashrams of Vrindavan, and the divine heritage of Ayodhya.

Day 1 begins in Mathura — the birthplace of Lord Krishna. You will explore Shri Krishna Janmasthan Temple, Dwarkadhish Temple, Vishram Ghat, and then proceed to Vrindavan for darshan at Banke Bihari Temple, ISKCON Temple, Nidhivan, Seva Kunj, and Prem Mandir.

Day 2 leads to Ayodhya, the birthplace of Lord Ram. Highlights include Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan, Saryu Ghat, and the divine Saryu River aarti.

Day 3 concludes with peaceful sightseeing in Ayodhya and a smooth drop back to Delhi. Organized by BrajBhoomi Tour & Travels, this tour offers private AC transport, optional guide services, and full support — ensuring a safe, comfortable, and spiritually fulfilling experience.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 — Mathura & Vrindavan Sightseeing

  • Pickup from Delhi / Mathura & transfer to Mathura.
  • Darshan at Shri Krishna Janmasthan Temple & Dwarkadhish Temple.
  • Visit Vishram Ghat (optional boat ride).
  • Proceed to Vrindavan — Banke Bihari Temple, ISKCON, Nidhivan & Seva Kunj.
  • Evening at Prem Mandir with Musical Fountain Show.
  • Overnight stay in Mathura / Vrindavan.

Day 2 — Ayodhya Temple Visits

  • Early morning departure for Ayodhya.
  • Visit Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple.
  • Explore Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan & Saryu Ghat.
  • Witness the divine Saryu Aarti (evening).
  • Overnight stay in Ayodhya.

Day 3 — Final Darshan & Return to Delhi

  • Morning darshan at local Ayodhya temples.
  • Breakfast & checkout.
  • Return journey to Delhi / Mathura.
  • Tour ends with blessings & beautiful memories.
