Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Local Agra Tour

  • Pickup from Agra station, airport, or your hotel.
  • Visit the majestic Taj Mahal (sunset view).
  • Explore Agra Fort — UNESCO heritage site.
  • Local market tour (handicrafts, leather, marble work).
  • Check-in at hotel & overnight stay in Agra.

Day 2 — Sunrise Taj + Fatehpur Sikri

  • Sunrise visit to the Taj Mahal for incredible photography.
  • Drive to Fatehpur Sikri — explore Buland Darwaza & Salim Chishti Dargah.
  • Lunch and return drop to Agra/Delhi.
