Package Overview

Journey through spiritual landscapes — start with Braj’s devotion in Mathura & Vrindavan, experience Agra's heritage, and end with peace and Ganga aartis in Haridwar & Rishikesh.

The Haridwar Rishikesh Tour Package via Agra, Mathura & Vrindavan is a unique blend of devotion, culture, and heritage crafted for travelers seeking spiritual depth and iconic experiences in northern India. Starting from Delhi, this itinerary visits the sacred towns of Mathura and Vrindavan — where you will partake in temple darshans including Shri Krishna Janmasthan, Banke Bihari Temple, ISKCON and Prem Mandir. The journey then proceeds to Agra for the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort, before taking you to the peaceful banks of the Ganges in Haridwar and the yogic ambiance of Rishikesh.

Along the way, enjoy comfortable private AC transfers, optional knowledgeable guides, and carefully selected stays. In Haridwar and Rishikesh, participate in the evening Ganga Aarti, yoga sessions (optional), and riverside relaxation. This package is ideal for pilgrims, couples, families, and small groups who want a balanced tour combining spiritual practices, cultural exploration, and comfortable travel.

Organized by BrajBhoomi Tour & Travels, the package includes local coordination, safe transfers, and support throughout the itinerary — designed to give you meaningful darshans, calm river experiences and memorable cultural interactions without rush.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 — Delhi → Mathura / Vrindavan

  • Early morning pickup from Delhi and drive to Mathura.
  • Visit Shri Krishna Janmasthan Temple, Dwarkadhish Temple and Vishram Ghat.
  • Proceed to Vrindavan — Banke Bihari Temple, ISKCON and Prem Mandir evening visit.
  • Overnight stay in Mathura / Vrindavan.

Day 2 — Mathura / Vrindavan → Agra

  • Morning at leisure in Vrindavan with optional morning aarti.
  • Drive to Agra; visit Agra Fort and local markets.
  • Evening free; overnight stay in Agra.

Day 3 — Agra → Haridwar

  • Sunrise visit to the Taj Mahal (optional sunrise timing).
  • After breakfast, drive towards Haridwar (long drive with breaks).
  • Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri — an unforgettable spiritual experience.
  • Overnight in Haridwar.

Day 4 — Haridwar → Rishikesh (Local exploration)

  • Short transfer to Rishikesh (30–60 mins depending on location).
  • Visit Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, Triveni Ghat and attend evening Ganga Aarti (if scheduled).
  • Optional yoga class or Ganga rafting (advance booking recommended).
  • Overnight in Rishikesh.

Day 5 — Rishikesh → Return to Delhi

  • Morning leisure or yoga session.
  • Breakfast and checkout; drive back to Delhi with drop-off.
  • Tour concludes with blessings and fond memories.
